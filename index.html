About

What is Mental Health?

Mental health describes our emotional, psychological and social wellbeing. It affects how we think, feel and act in everyday life. Good mental health helps us cope with challenges, build healthy relationships and make informed decisions.

Mental health exists on a spectrum, and everyone experiences changes in their wellbeing throughout life. Seeking support when needed is a positive step towards maintaining good mental health.

Common Challenges

Stress

Stress is a natural response to pressure, but prolonged stress can affect both physical and emotional health. Learning healthy coping strategies can help reduce its impact.

Anxiety

Feeling anxious from time to time is normal. However, persistent anxiety that interferes with daily activities may require additional support.

Low Mood

Everyone experiences periods of sadness. If feelings of low mood continue for an extended period or begin affecting daily life, professional advice may be beneficial.

Self Care Tips

Look after your wellbeing

  • Maintain a regular sleep routine.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated.
  • Take regular physical excercise.
  • Spend time with supportive friends and family.
  • Practice mindfulness or relaxation techniques
  • Take regular breaks from work or study.
  • Limit excessive social media use when it negatively affects your mood.
  • Reach out for help when you need it.
Small positive actions each day can make a meaningful difference
