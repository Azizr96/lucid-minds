What is Mental Health?

Mental health describes our emotional, psychological and social wellbeing. It affects how we think, feel and act in everyday life. Good mental health helps us cope with challenges, build healthy relationships and make informed decisions.

Mental health exists on a spectrum, and everyone experiences changes in their wellbeing throughout life. Seeking support when needed is a positive step towards maintaining good mental health.
